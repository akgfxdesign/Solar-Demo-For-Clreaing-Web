The Protocol

The 6-Step Power Wash

Solar cleaning is electrical maintenance, not janitorial work. We follow a strict protocol to ensure safety and performance.

01

Health Audit

We scan the array for hot-spots, micro-cracks, and loose cabling before applying any water.

02

Ion-Wash

Application of de-ionized water to neutralize static charge and loosen surface dust.

03

Soft-Scrub

Gentle mechanical cleaning using rotating soft brushes and PH-neutral biodegradable soap.

04

Deep-Target

Focused removal of stubborn bird droppings, lichen, and industrial soot deposits.

05

Pure-Rinse

Final rinse with purified water to ensure a zero-spot, streak-free glass finish.

06

Yield Audit

We verify the immediate jump in power output at the inverter level.

Expert Insights

Answers to your technical questions.

How often should I clean my solar panels?

We recommend cleaning every 3 months (quarterly). In dusty industrial areas like Ambad/Satpur MIDC, monthly cleaning might be required.

Why do I need bird proofing?

Birds love nesting under solar panels. Their droppings are acidic and can damage panels, while nests can cause overheating and fire risks. Our proofing mesh prevents this.

What else do you clean?

Besides solar panels, we offer gutter cleaning to prevent water damage and pressure washing for driveways, patios, and industrial floors.
